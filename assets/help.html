Q: How do I turn VolumeSwipe on or off?
A: Tap on the box to the left of "Active".

Q: How do I boost the volume?
A: Put a checkmark in the box to the left of "Boost", and then you can swipe up to 140% (be careful of potential damage to speakers, headphones or hearing).

Q: How do I use VolumeSwipe with other applications?

A: Option 1: If you can see the system status bar, you can swipe down from it to see notifications, then tap on 'VolumeSwipe', and then you can adjust the volume in VolumeSwipe, and press BACK to go back. You can also email arpruss@gmail.com to add additional applications.
Option 2: Press the MENU key in VolumeSwipe, choose "Settings", and put a checkmark in the box beside "Active in all apps".
